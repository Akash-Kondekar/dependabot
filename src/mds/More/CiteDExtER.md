Examples of possible wording for inclusion in your publication acknowledgements are listed below:

-   The study design and data extraction were facilitated using the Dexter software.
-   The steps involved in the study design and data extraction were facilitated by the Dexter Software.
-   The study design based data extraction was conducted using Dexter tool.
